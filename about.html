About Noe

Hello my name is Noe (Noe is also the name of the pink mist that One can see at dawn or evenings looking up the sky)

I am an independent New York licensed massage therapist, who specializes in Hawaiian Lomi Kino massage.The lomi Massage I practice is called the Iwi 'Ula style Lomi After my Kumu(teacher).

I started my journey of learning about native Hawaiian culture from Kumi in 2003.In 2010 I began my Lomi Kino training.My work is my passion,I am very honored to work width clientele to help promote their health and lifestyle .I am so grateful for all of the appreciation my clients expressed to me.
